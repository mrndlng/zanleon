The Commonwealth Times is a weekly, independent broadsheet published by the VCU Student Media Center. Three other designers and I worked together on every step of the process: communicating with the editorial team, brainstorming visual concepts, page designs and layouts for various sections, creating infographics and illustration, typsetting, pairing visual and written content, setting up master pages, preparing files for print, sending the paper to the printer and communicating with vendors.
