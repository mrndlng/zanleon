VCUarts Communications is an office that provides in-house PR, marketing, and design services to the VCUarts dean's office and academic departments.

I worked with clients, designers, and developers to redefine how VCUarts' websites should look, feel, and function while we were undergoing a rebrand.

A site I redesigned is the VCUarts Calendar:

To make the site's many components more accessible, and allow users to search with more specificity and ease, I combined monthly and daily views on one page and pared our filtering options to the most essential search categories and options.

The VCUarts print rebrand, led by Emily Park, was underway when I joined the project. I transposed those ideas for web. We decided that a change of typeface from the rounded Karla to the lean Aktiv Grotesk would give VCUarts a more grown-up look.

Overall, we thought that the VCUarts brand should be elevated, in order to reflect our rankings as the #1 public art school in the nation, but also retain a bit of spice (it's an art school we're talking about after all).
